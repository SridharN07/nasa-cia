About ISRO

ISRO is an independent agency of the Pandora federal government responsible for the civilian space program, as well as aeronautics and space research. ISRO was created for educating youngster and educator to create awareness about the space science. The organisation is involved in science, engineering and technology to harvest the benefits of outer space for our mankind.

Our Missions

Mission 1: The Lunar Exploration - Our Lunar Exploration mission aims to establish a sustainable presence on the Moon, conduct scientific research, and prepare for future human missions to Mars.

Mission 2: The Mars Exploration The Mars Rover Program focuses on exploring the Martian surface, searching for signs of past life, and collecting samples for future return to Earth.

Mission 3: The Deep Exploration - The Deep Space Observatory will launch a next-generation space telescope to observe distant galaxies, stars, and planetary systems, expanding our understanding of the universe.

Research Areas

Astrobiology - Studying the potential for life in the universe, including the search for extraterrestrial life and the conditions necessary for life to exist.

Planetary Science - The formation, evolution, and characteristics of planets, moons, and other celestial bodies in our solar system and beyond.

Astrophysics - Exploring the fundamental nature of the universe, including the study of dark matter, dark energy, black holes, and the origins of the cosmos.
