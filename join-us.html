Eligibility Criteria

For Undergraduate:

  1. Students who want to purse Astronomy courses must have passed Class 12 in PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) of the Science Stream with a minimum of 50% of marks from a recognized university.
  2. The criteria vary across different universities and colleges.
  3. Reputed colleges conduct their test; students have to qualify the entrance exam to take admission.

For Postgraduate:

  1. Minimum eligibility is to hold a B.Tech/ M.Sc. degree related to space in a recognized institution.
  2. The minimal pass percentile is 45% or any other equivalent qualification.
  3. Some of the big colleges conduct entrance examinations for admission to the postgraduate course.


Programmes Outcomes

    Learning space science offers numerous benefits and can lead to a variety of positive outcomes:
  1. Enhanced Understanding of the Universe: Students gain a deeper appreciation and understanding of the cosmos, including the origins of the universe, the solar system, and the potential for life beyond Earth.
  2. Critical Thinking and Problem-Solving Skills: Space science often involves complex problems that require innovative solutions, helping students develop strong analytical and critical thinking skills.
  3. Inspiration and Curiosity: The vastness and mysteries of space can inspire a lifelong passion for learning and exploration, encouraging students to ask questions and seek answers.
  4. Technological and Scientific Literacy: Students become familiar with advanced technologies and scientific principles, which are applicable in many fields beyond space science.
  5. Career Opportunities: Knowledge of space science can open doors to careers in astronomy, astrophysics, aerospace engineering, and other related fields.
  6. Global Perspective: Studying space science fosters a sense of global citizenship and awareness of our place in the universe, promoting international collaboration and understanding.
  7. Innovation and Creativity: The challenges of space exploration require creative thinking and innovation, skills that are valuable in any career.
  8. STEM Integration: Space science naturally integrates various STEM disciplines, providing a comprehensive educational experience that enhances overall STEM proficiency.
