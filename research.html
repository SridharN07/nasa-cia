Our Research Areas

Interstellar Space and Research Organisation - 2024
Sl. No Research Area Name Description
01 Astrobiology Studying the potential for life in the universe, including the search for extraterrestrial life and the conditions necessary for life to exist.
02 Planetary Science The formation, evolution, and characteristics of planets, moons, and other celestial bodies in our solar system and beyond.
03 Astrophysics Exploring the fundamental nature of the universe, including the study of dark matter, dark energy, black holes, and the origins of the cosmos.
04 Space Technology Developing new technologies for space exploration, such as advanced propulsion systems, robotics, and life support systems.
05 Satellite Communications Enhancing global communication networks through the deployment and management of satellite constellations.
06 Space Manufacturing Exploring techniques for manufacturing and assembling structures in space, which could revolutionize space missions and habitats.
07 Space Tourism Investigating the potential for commercial space travel and the infrastructure needed to support it
08 Planets Observation Using satellites to monitor and analyze Different Planet's climate, weather patterns, and natural disasters
09 Space Medicine Researching the effects of space travel on the human body and developing medical technologies to support long-duration missions
